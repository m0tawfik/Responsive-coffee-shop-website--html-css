ABOUT US

Our Story:

COFFEE HAVEN is the culmination of a captivating journey that began with a shared passion for the finest coffee and an unyielding curiosity to explore the depths of its allure. It was a realization that coffee is not just a beverage, it's a portal to a world of cultures, stories, and experiences waiting to be discovered.

Our journey was sparked by the collective vision of our founders passionate adventurers, tireless explorers, and ardent coffee enthusiasts. With backgrounds as diverse as the beans they sought, they united under the banner of Gorill & Coffee to embark on a daring expedition,

As avid travelers, they traversed remote coffee-growing regions, navigating the misty landscapes of Ethiopia, the vibrant plantations of Colombia, and the rich highlands of Kenya.
